Parakeet Care Guide

  • Provide it a spacious, caged habitat with many places to perch
  • Line the bottom of the habitat with habitat paper and replace regularly to remove droppings
  • Give your bird plenty of toys to play with
  • Parakeets must have their toenails clipped and their feathers groomed
  • Parakeets like to eat seeds and fruit (store-bought pellets will suffice); they need water to drink
  • Parakeets frequently bathe in water
  • Apparently, people have microorganisms in their mouths which make parakeets sick, so do not feed your parakeet from your mouth!
  • They live around ten years
